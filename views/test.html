About QANDOS

Empowering Secure Real Estate Investments

Our Story

We were once meeting with a friend who had migrated to Canada with his family, and he shared stories of how a lot of Nigerians in Diaspora had fallen victim to investment scams even in the hands of friends, family and relatives. This was mostly particular to real estate-related investments.

When we dug deeper, we realized that one of the major factors deterring a lot of Africans in diaspora from investing back home was the unavailability of a trusted and reliable platform they can feel safe with when making those real estate investment decisions.

Mission and Vision

Our Mission

To make real estate investment safe, easy, transparent and secure.

Our Vision

We are working to see more Nigerians and Africans in Diaspora feel safe and secure about investing in real estate back home.

Why QANDOS?
